PUGILASONIC SOCIETY
Digital Initiation Protocol – Entered Apprentice Combatant
(Current as of November 2025)
Who We Are
A global brotherhood of men who train and compete in combat sports and who live by discipline, respect, charity, and lifelong learning.
Open to adult men (18+) of good character from every background: laborers, tradesmen, businessmen, soldiers, doctors, programmers, professional fighters, cybersecurity operators — anyone with a real passion for the fighting arts.
Belief in a Higher Power is required (any personal definition; affirmed during application).
Separate divisions exist for:
– Contact Sports (football, rugby, hockey, etc.)
– Digital Warfare (cybersecurity operators – CTF league & war games)
How to Join

Go to pugilasonic.com → “Join”
Fill out the application form
Complete a short 5–10 minute video interview
Pass a light background check (we run it)
Affirm belief in a Higher Power (one click)
Upload proof of active training
• Minimum 3 minutes total raw footage (sparring, rolling, competition, pads, wrestling – face visible, no edits or music)
Upload your Official Member Photo
• Black rashguard or black T-shirt with small red Society emblem (left chest)
• Red 16-oz gloves on both hands + white hand wraps visible
• Plain background, good lighting
• This becomes your permanent roster photo
Complete the five mandatory online courses (~12 hours total)
• History of Combat Sports
• Fighter Ethics & Responsibility
• Brain Health & Concussion Protocol
• Charity & Community Service
• Pugilasonic Society Constitution
(Cybersecurity applicants only) Complete the introductory CTF module and submit one flag/write-up for Digital Warfare Division access

Once approved, you will receive the Initiation Kit in a plain black box.
Initiation Kit Contents
– Black blindfold hood
– One red 16-oz training glove
– One pair white 160-inch hand wraps
– Small brass bell (identical for every member worldwide)
– White cable tow cord (~90 cm)
– Oath card + pre-paid return envelope
– Official black rashguard or T-shirt with red emblem (your size)
The Digital Initiation
A 30–40 minute private video call with current members.
The sequence is simple and serious:
– Preparation with hood, cable tow, and glove over heart
– Three symbolic strikes thrown at the camera (no flinch)
– Oath spoken once, clearly
– Oath card signed on camera and mailed back
– Simultaneous ringing of the brass bells (the Resonance)
– Hands wrapped on camera
– Signs, grip, and word demonstrated
– Welcome by the brotherhood
After Initiation
– Lifetime digital membership card + unique member number issued within 12 hours
– Immediate access to the encrypted global roster, Signal/Telegram groups, training vault, worldwide gym discounts, and (if qualified) the Digital Warfare CTF server
– First duty: within 120 days upload one new continuous 3+ minute live training or competition clip
– Annual duties: complete at least one continuing-education module and contribute (time or money) to the Society charity drive
